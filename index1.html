What do we do here?

As a non-profit organisation based in Singapore, our mission is to create a bridge of communication between students in Singapore and China to improve the education and general welfare of children in China. We are collaborating with Stepping Stones China to better support our beneficiarie.

What is the profile of our beneficiaries?

Our main beneficiaries include migrant children & youth, rural children & youth, left-behind children, children and youth from low-income families in Shanghai, children and youth with special needs and English teachers from rural and migrant schools.

How do our actions affect our beneficiaries?

As a non-profit organisation, our mission is to create a bridge of communication between students in Singapore and China. We hope more students can work with us to create an impact foe these Chinese students.

What can our Singapore volunteers gain?

Our Singapore volunteers will be nurtured holistically in the sharing process, appreciating diversity and developing into bilingual talents. They became more informed about the educational landscape in China, turning into compassionate and thoughtful individuals.

Requirements for volunteers

We look forward to recruiting volunteers aged 15 and above who are fluent in both Chinese and English.
